Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science could be applied to multiple fields ranging from the medical field all the way to the 
Oil industry. Within the medical field, data science can help with the analysis of patient's data to further
the advancement of research. This can help improve treatments for diseases or even assist an individual's 
personal treatment. The information gathered from data science is essential for everything around us.
