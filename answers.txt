Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git allows you to alter different repositories without affecting the main source. Multiple people can
work on the same project with Git. It also allows for the main owner of the project to ensure a change is 
necessary prior to merging it with Main. It's an essential tool for group projects and every day coding. 

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science could be applied to multiple fields ranging from the medical field all the way to the 
Oil industry. Within the medical field, data science can help with the analysis of patient's data to further
the advancement of research. This can help improve treatments for diseases or even assist an individual's 
personal treatment. The information gathered from data science is essential for everything around us. 

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the history of the previous branches by combining them into a single branch and
creating a new merge commit. Rebasing places the history of the changes of one branch infront of the history
of another branch. This makes rebasing harder to revert compared to merging.
